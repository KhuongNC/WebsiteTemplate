Software Development for Toshiba Products

Overview

Toshiba is a diversified electric/electronic manufacturer and provides a wide range of products and services on a global basis. As one of global software development centers of Toshiba corporate, TSDV provides software development services in variety of Toshiba products & solutions, classified in following business domains:

  • Energy Systems & Solutions
  • Infrastructure Systems & Solutions
  • Electronic Devices & Storage Solutions
  • Digital Solutions

Energy System & Solution

Power systems are indispensable for contemporary lifestyles, and demand for them is expected to remain vigorous worldwide. Leveraging expertise cultivated since the company’s foundation, Toshiba is contributing to the creation of infrastructure that enriches quality of life for people throughout the world. In order to ensure a stable supply of electricity, we offer hydro, solar, geothermal and wind power generation facilities as renewable energies (source: Toshiba Asia)

In business domain of Energy Systems & Solutions, TSDV has been contributing to a wide range of software that is applied in the field: from monitoring & control applications in power plants (HMI), engineering/configuration tools for electric engineers, to firmware, software platform for electrical devices/equipment of transmission & distribution.

Project samples & corresponding key programming technologies:

  • Develop firmware & HMI for Toshiba industrial protection relay (C, C++ MFC, uITRON/Windows)
  • Develop server & HMI for SCADA of high/medium/low voltage systems (C, C++, Java, Linux)
  • Develop visualization web-based components for monitoring & control power plants (ReactJS, NodeJS, Microservices, AWS, Docker)

Infrastructure System & Solutions

Toshiba aims to achieve a sustainable society that is safe, secure and reliable through our solutions for Social & Industrial Infrastructure and Building Facilities all over the world. For Social & Industrial Infrastructure, we offer systems and solutions for water supply and sewerage, highways, disaster prevention, broadcasting, power distribution, telecommunications, security & automation, railways and general industry. For Building Facilities, we offer energy-saving and eco-friendly solutions for elevators, escalators, air-conditioners and lightings that promise greater comfort. (source: Toshiba Asia)

In business domain of Social Infrastructure, TSDV has been contributing to variety of software in almost all range of Toshiba products mentioned above. Typical software is: HMI for monitoring and control for industrial systems & community solutions, engineering/configuration tools for operators/domain specific engineers, firmware, software platform for devices/equipment in social infrastructure systems.

Typical development projects & corresponding key programming technologies:

  • Developing platform/control software of lane controller, lane monitoring in Toshiba Electronic Toll Collector (ETC) system (C, Multi-thread, Socket, Linux)
  • Developing IT solution of parcel monitoring & inspection in custom systems (Java Spring Boot, HTML/JS)
  • Developing HMI for broadcast monitoring systems in TV stations (C, C++, Java, HTML/JS, Linux)
  • Developing HMI for setting & controlling light scenes of Toshiba lighting devices (C# WPF, Java Android)
  • Developing HMI for monitoring energy consumption in a building (Java, HTML, JS, Objective C/Swift, iOS)
  • Developing control software for flip boding machines, vending machines, molding machines, etc. (C, C++, MFC, QT, C#, Unity, Java, PHP, PLC)
  • Develop remote monitoring and controlling system for Highway systems. (Java, Swing)

Electronic Devices & Storage Solutions

Creating technology that is intelligent — that takes thought, vision and forward thinking — that is what Toshiba is about. We work closely with our customers to create electronic components and storage products that are designed to excite the marketplace and deliver on our shared vision of success. We have spent decades developing strong relationships and applying our resources to your challenges. Toshiba is committed to work with you every step of the way in developing your product from design to delivery to ensure your success. (source: Toshiba Asia)

TSDV is now contributing to the automotive and storage business of Toshiba by developing and providing solution services based on Toshiba ARM-processor-based MCU, and enhancing performance of the products by improving the algorithm. In detail, TSDV is creating the Low Level Driver; providing quality service to customer; enhancing performance of firmware/driver/database; prototyping the solutions of IoT; creating models for system engineering.

Typical development projects & corresponding key programming technologies:

  • Developing the Low-Level Drivers for Automotive MCU
  • Developing the AUTOSAR Microcontroller Abstraction Layer (MCAL) for Automotive MCU.
  • Refactoring design and porting drivers for Storage devices
  • Creating models for every internal-object inside Toshiba MCU and external-object to demonstrate the smart/intelligent of Toshiba MCU
  • Porting and develop IoT node in Smart City, GPS Tracking system, Smart Home
  • Creating the Windows driver, porting firmware for product in Social Infrastructure/Factory Automation. Enhancing the performance of Database system of Energy System
  • Providing technical training service like basic embedded software development; support Toshiba to transfer technical solution to local customers in Vietnam.

Digital Solutions

The Internet of Things (IoT), the connection of diverse things to the Internet, is creating new business based on utilization of immense volumes of data. By promoting “Things x ICT x People”, we are creating value and a brighter future with our customers and partners.

Through ‘Co-Creation’ that extends far beyond the boundaries of company, industry and region, we are working to achieve a safe, secure and harmonious society — where people all over the world can live in comfort and safety (source: Toshiba Asia)

TSDV is now contributing to the development of Framework/platform for IoT Big Data Storage & Cloud Services dedicated for Toshiba

Project samples & corresponding key programming technologies

  • Developing for the Database of IoT/Big Data (Java, JS, Spring Boot, Azure)
  • Developing for IoT remote monitoring and visualization service (JS, Spring Boot, Azure)

Besides that, we are also using our strength of Web based technology to joining in development of solutions package for many business domains or industry such as Manufacturing, Logistic, Distribution, Media, Government, Human Resource Management, Customer Relationship Management.

Project samples & corresponding key programming technologies

  • Developing health-care data collection and analysis system (Java, Spring Boot, .NET, JS, Angular, AWS, Azure)
  • Developing Data management system and newspapers designing support system (MFC, WPF, Azure)
  • Developing for the Human Resource management package (Java, JS, Selenium)
  • Developing file secure sending system which uses quantum key distribution (Java, Shell Script, JS, Spring Boot)
  • Developing Business management systems which provides services related to Toshiba AI System
