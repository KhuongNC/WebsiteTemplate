Senior AI Developer

Job Description

We are looking for excellent AI engineers who can research & develop AI solutions for a variety of business domains of Toshiba. You will be given chances of working with different types of AI topics in Computer Vision, Time-Series Data Analytics, Natural Language Processing, Speech Recognition, and Optimization. You will work with latest technologies in the field. You will sharpen your skills by constantly sharing with other senior members in our team, collaborating with outside researchers in Toshiba R&D world-wide centers or attending international conferences/workshops. The working environment is open, flexible, collaborative, encouraging and excellence oriented. Senior candidates with proven leadership & management skills will have an opportunity to lead a team of around 05 members.

Education & Experiences

  • Bachelor’s degree (Master or PhD is an advantage) in Computer Science/Information Technology, Electrical/Telecommunication or related Engineering fields, Mathematics.
  • At least 03-year working experiences (for Senior level) in real projects of one or more areas below:
    • Computer Vision
    • Time-Series Data Analytics
    • Natural Language Processing
    • Speech Recognition
    • Optimization

 

Knowledge/Skills

  • Solid background knowledge at Maths (such as Linear Algebra, Probability & Statistics, Optimization)
  • Having practical knowledge with one of areas: machine learning, deep learning, data science, and optimization (Familiar with latest Deep Learning techniques is preferred)
  • Good at one of programming languages: Python, C++, C#, Java (Python is most preferred.)
  • Good at one of Deep Learning frameworks: TensorFlow/Keras or PyTorch and Python libraries (for example: scikit-learn, numpy, scipy, pandas, matplotlib, OpenCV)
  • Ability to read and implement research papers quickly and apply ideas to related problems.
  • Strong analytical skills and mindset.
  • Good at writing technical reports and presentation of technical problems (in English).
  • Good at English (all 04 skills), knowing Japanese language is a plus.
  • Other attitude characteristics: proactive, responsible, can-do, and get-thing-done.

BENEFIT & PERKS

  • Salary: Negotiable.
  • High Remuneration Package (up to 15 months salary) and Full Social, Medical & Unemployment Insurance
  • Full salary in probation
  • Work in well - equipped and professional IT environment and IT community;
  • To be trained and/or instructed by Japanese Specialists in Vietnam as well as in Japan and others;
  • Become member of a giant corporation;
  • Many opportunity to work/study core technology of Toshiba (including OJT long term in Japan) ;
