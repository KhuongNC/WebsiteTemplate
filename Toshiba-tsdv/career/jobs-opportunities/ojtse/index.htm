On Job Training

JOB DESCRIPTION

Selected candidates will have many opportunities to gain experience and develop a career at TSDV according to the following roadmap:

  • Basic Training Stage (1 months): Members are given a foundation but comprehensive course on software development process and related techniques (Algorithm Data Structures, OOP , UML, design analysis, software testing, etc) with experienced engineers and professors from top engineering universities.
  • Project Training Stage (at least 6 months): Candidates who pass this stage will be able to participate in real projects together with official software engineers at TSDV. Trained in programming techniques, experienced all steps in the development process and learned knowledge of the application field as well as teamwork and project management skills
  • Candidates have good ability and good working attitude will be shifted to official members in company by the day after graduation without joining the recruitment process.
  • You will join one of projects which have varied tech stack:
    • Web Applications:  Java Spring, Python, NodeJS, ReactJS, Angular, VueJS
    • Desktop Applications: Java Core, C/C++, C#, Winform.
    • C Embedded

REQUIREMENTS

  • Last year students (graduates in 2022 - Early 2023) majoring in Information Technology, Computer Science, Computer Science, Software Engineering, Electronics and Telecommunications ... or related majors.
  • Have to join at least 2.5 days/ week at company (flexible on your study schedule)
  • Has a good background in engineering fields related to Computer Science such as Mathematics, Physics, Boolean logic, computer architecture, Statistical probability, etc.
  • Master the basic knowledge of Object Oriented Programming and one of the languages ​​C / C ++, Java, C #.
  • Eager to learn, obey discipline & be responsible for the job.
  • Good at English reading and writing.

BENEFITS & PERKS

**Income

  • Basic training period (1 month): 3,300,000 VND / month.
  • Project Training Stage (at least 6 months): Based on your working time, up to 7,500,000 VND / month if you join 44 sessions / month. You need to join training at least 2.5 days/ week flexible on your study schedule.

** Other benefits:

  • Become official staff right after graduation if your performance is good.
  • Fully trained in software development knowledge and quality assurance by experienced engineers and lecturers at top universities in Vietnam.
  • To be directly involved in the development stages in a real software development project, using advanced technology, under the guidance of experienced engineers.
  • Experience the Japanese working spirit: honesty, professional, always improving, attaching great importance to quality and core value for themselves and customers.
  • Join a youthful, friendly, respectful and supportive working environment at work, with many team building activities (company trip once a year, football club, dragon ball, swimming, running, gym, yoga, etc).
