Business Domain Overview

Software Development for Toshiba Products

Toshiba is a diversified electric/electronic manufacturer and provides a wide range of products and services on a global basis.

As one of global software development centers of Toshiba corporate, TSDV provides software development services in variety of Toshiba products & solutions

Check details

Fundamental Software Development

As a member of Toshiba Corporate Software Engineering & Technology Center (SWC), TSDV has a mission to contribute to the development of common technology for improving the productivity and quality of software development in Toshiba group, and the enhancement of software design capabilities, development capabilities, and quality capabilities

Check details

Research for Advanced IT

Recently, at TSDV, we have established Advanced IT Research Department. As Toshiba is aiming to become a leading company of Cyber Physics System (CPS) in which AI is one of the core parts, TSDV’s AI Center has a great mission to contribute to this technology movement. We solve practical problems emerged from Toshiba’s manufacturing and business by applying latest machine learning and data analytics techniques. Our work adds values to customer products and user experience.

Check details

LSI Design & Verification

Toshiba is one of LSI manufacturing companies in Japan

TSDV established LSI Design & Development department to contribute resources and cope with the various areas. Through close collaborating with Japan side in training, our center‘s mission is to develop the human resource for LSI design domain and to support strongly to customers by bringing their products to the market in terms of upstream processes of IC design and the supply of prototypes.

Check details

Software Testing Services

Besides development & research activities, TSDV has established Test Center since 2014 dedicated for independent testing services.

Our target is to become Center of Excellence of Software Testing in Toshiba Group by providing effective testing services, helping to build up high quality products before release to market
